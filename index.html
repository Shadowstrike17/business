üìä Case Study: Doha Food Store

Statement of Comprehensive Income

The Doha Food Store sells a wide range of groceries. It is located in a suburb of Doha in Qatar and serves local residents. Musab Assad and his family own the business, and his three sons are employed in the store. Below is the financial data for the year ending 31 May 2015:

  • Revenue: US$ 256,400
  • Cost of sales: US$ 137,100
  • Gross profit: US$ 119,300
  • Expenses: US$ 77,900
  • Operating profit: US$ 41,400
  • Finance costs: US$ 600
  • Profit for the year: US$ 40,800
